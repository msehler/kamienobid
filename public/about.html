About us

Finding a lawyer should feel clearer from the start.

Kamieno exists to make the first step of getting legal help less opaque. Instead of contacting firms one by one, clients can post a matter once and compare proposals from verified lawyers before deciding who to engage.

What we believe

Clients should be able to compare before they commit.

Lawyers should win work by showing relevance, clarity, and approach.

The first step of legal help should feel more informed and less intimidating.

How it works

Kamieno helps people brief a legal issue once, then compare real responses from eligible lawyers.

Post once

A client explains the matter once, adds the key documents, and gives lawyers enough context to respond in a meaningful way.

Compare proposals

Verified lawyers respond with pricing and a proposed approach, so clients can compare options side by side instead of guessing who might be the best fit.

Choose with confidence

The client reviews the field first and decides who to engage after seeing the available options, not before.

Built for trust

The product is shaped around relevance, verification, and a better first decision for the client.

Verified local lawyers

Lawyers only bid where they hold the right practising credentials, so clients are hearing from lawyers who are eligible to act in the relevant market.

Strategy-led proposals

Proposals are meant to show thinking, not just interest, so the comparison is more useful from the first response.

Localised by market

Prompts, required information, and legal framing adapt by jurisdiction so the experience makes sense for where the matter is being posted.
