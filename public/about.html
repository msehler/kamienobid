About us

One legal problem. Several real options.

Kamieno is a legal bidding platform where individuals and businesses can post a legal matter once and receive proposals from verified local lawyers, each explaining their likely approach and pricing up front.

Why it matters

Clients should be able to compare before they commit.

Lawyers should win work by showing their thinking, not just their name.

Legal help should feel clearer, faster, and less opaque at the first step.

What Kamieno does

Kamieno is designed to make the first step of engaging a lawyer more transparent.

Post once

A client describes their matter once, selects the relevant practice area, and uploads the supporting information needed for lawyers to understand the issue.

Compare proposals

Verified lawyers respond with a structured proposal that includes an initial strategy and pricing, so the client can compare options side by side.

Choose with confidence

Instead of contacting firms one by one, the client reviews competing approaches first and decides who to engage only after seeing the field.

Built around the brief

The product has been shaped around comparison, verification, and jurisdiction-aware intake.

Verified local lawyers

Lawyers only bid in the jurisdictions where they hold the relevant practising rights, so clients see responses from lawyers who are actually eligible to act.

Strategy-led proposals

The brief requires lawyers to provide a tailored initial strategy, not just a generic expression of interest, so the comparison is substantive from the start.

Localised by market

Currency, prompts, legal language, required documents, and disclaimers adapt by jurisdiction so the experience feels appropriate to where the client is posting.

Launch focus

The early product is being shaped for English-speaking common-law markets, starting with the launch set named in the brief.

The current scope is centred on Australia, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom first, with the wider model built to support the United States, Canada, and Ireland as the platform expands.

Across those markets, the aim is consistent: one posting flow for clients, free bidding for lawyers, and a cleaner way to compare legal options before an engagement begins.
